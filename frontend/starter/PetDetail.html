Moneymaker, Shiba Inu

Moneymaker may sound like a business mogul, but don’t let the name fool you—this Shiba Inu is all heart and fluff. Moneymaker is a clever little fox with a signature smirk and an independent spirit. He’s curious, alert, and always watching the world like he’s making mental stock trades.

He loves puzzle toys, crunchy treats, and a good sunbathing session in the yard. He can be a bit aloof with strangers at first, but once you’re in his circle, you’re in for life. Ideal for experienced dog owners, Moneymaker will be your loyal shadow—on his terms, of course.

Vaccinated, neutered, and ready to join a home where he's respected and adored like the savvy soul he is.
